Description

A unique Rose Muscat wine of ruby colour. Pungent cherry aromas give way to a smell that can only be described as a bunch of red roses on Valentines day!

Tasting notes: the bouquet of rose petals with spice & honey tints. The palate is full-bodied, well balanced, fresh, spicy.

Pairing suggestions: can be paired with lamb & poultry dishes, especially sweeter ones. Spicy dishes (Thai style). Strong white cheeses such as Brie & Camembert, as well as olives. As a dessert by itself.
