Description

This is a classic Crimean Muscat, made from top quality White Muscat grapes grown on the south Crimean coast, & has the distinctive Muscat aroma and taste.

Tasting notes: South Coast White Muscat is a high quality dessert white wine of light amber colour with golden tints. Bright bouquet of flowers & honey. Full, balanced & delicate palate. Long pleasant aftertaste.

Pairing suggestions: grapes & all fresh fruit, as well as cheese & olives.  As a dessert by itself.
